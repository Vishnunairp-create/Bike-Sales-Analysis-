# Bike-Sales-Analysis-


This project presents an **interactive Bike Sales Dashboard built using Microsoft Power BI** to analyze sales performance across different regions, products, and customer segments. The goal of this dashboard is to transform raw sales data into meaningful insights that support data-driven decision making.

The dashboard provides a clear overview of key performance indicators such as total sales, revenue trends, regional sales distribution, and product performance. With interactive visuals and slicers, users can explore sales patterns and identify factors that influence business performance.

### Key Insights

* Overall bike sales performance and revenue trends
* Regional sales comparison
* Product category performance
* Customer and sales distribution analysis
* Time-based sales trends


## **Project Summary**

The **Bike Sales Dashboard Project** focuses on transforming raw sales data into meaningful insights using Power BI. The dataset was cleaned, structured, and modeled to enable efficient analysis and visualization.

The project involved building a data model, creating calculated measures, and designing interactive dashboards to track business performance. Filters and slicers allow users to drill down into specific regions, products, or time periods for deeper analysis.

### **Objectives**

* Monitor overall bike sales performance 📈
* Identify high-performing regions and products 🌍
* Analyze sales trends over time ⏳
* Support strategic sales and marketing decisions 🎯

### **Key Features**

* KPI cards for total sales and key metrics
* Regional sales analysis
* Product/category performance comparison
* Time-based trend analysis
* Interactive filters and slicers

### **Tools & Technologies**

* **Microsoft Power BI**
* Data Modeling
* DAX Measures
* Data Visualization
